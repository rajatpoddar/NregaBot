Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 05, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how NREGA Bot ("the Application") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring your data is secure.

1. Information We Collect

To provide our services, manage licenses, and offer cloud storage, we collect the following limited information:

  • Personal Information: Name, Email Address, Mobile Number, and Address details (Block, District, State, Pincode) provided during registration.
  • Device Information: A unique Machine ID (derived from your hardware) to lock your license key to your specific device(s) and prevent unauthorized use.
  • User Files: If you use the "Cloud Storage" or "File Manager" feature, the files you explicitly upload are stored on our secure servers.
  • Feedback & Support: Information you provide when contacting support or submitting feedback via the app.

2. Information We DO NOT Collect

We strictly respect the privacy of your official NREGA work. The Application DOES NOT collect, store, or transmit sensitive NREGA data to our servers:

  • Your NREGA login credentials (ID and Password).
  • Beneficiary details, Muster Roll data, FTO details, or any other data entered into the NREGA portal.
  • Browsing history outside of the automation context.

All automation tasks are performed locally on your computer. The data fetched from the NREGA website remains on your local machine.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used for:

  • License Management: To validate your trial or paid license and ensure it is active.
  • Service Delivery: To provide cloud storage features and sync files across your authorized devices.
  • Communication: To send important updates, license expiry reminders, and support responses (via Email or WhatsApp).
  • Security: To prevent fraud and abuse of the software license.

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data. Your personal information and files are stored on secure servers. License validation occurs over encrypted (HTTPS) connections. Payments are processed via Razorpay, and we do not store your credit card or banking details.

5. Cloud Storage

Files uploaded to the Cloud Storage are private to your account. You retain full ownership of your files. You are responsible for ensuring that you do not upload illegal or prohibited content.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be notified via email or an in-app notification. Continued use of the Application implies acceptance of the updated policy.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: nregabot@gmail.com

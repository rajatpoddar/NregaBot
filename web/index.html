Support

Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We've got answers.

Is my NREGA data safe?
Absolutely. The application works by automating your local browser. It only reads what's on the screen to perform its tasks. None of your NREGA data, work codes, or personal information is ever sent to our servers.
What happens after my 30-day free trial ends?
After 30 days, the automation features will be disabled. To continue using the app, you can support the project by purchasing a monthly, quarterly, or yearly license key from the "Pricing" section above.
How do I get a license key after purchasing?
After making a payment on our secure license page, your key will be generated instantly and sent to your registered email address within seconds. Check your spam folder if you don't see it.
Can I use one license key on multiple computers?
Each license key is valid for the number of devices you selected during purchase (1, 2, or 3). The license is locked to the specific computers it is first activated on. If you change your PC, please contact support to reset your key.
Why do I need to launch a browser from the app?
The app launches a browser (Chrome, Edge, or Firefox) with a special "debugging" connection. This secure connection allows NREGA Bot to fill forms and click buttons for you. Your regular browser window doesn't allow external control for security reasons.

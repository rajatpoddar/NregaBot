Cancellation & Refund Policy

Last updated: September 05, 2025

Thank you for supporting NREGA Bot. We strive to provide the best automation experience.

1. No Refunds Policy

All payments made for license keys (One-Time or Subscription) are generally non-refundable. Since we provide a comprehensive 30-day free trial with full features, we expect users to evaluate the software thoroughly before purchasing.

2. Subscription Cancellation

If you have subscribed to an auto-renewal plan:

  • You can cancel your subscription at any time.
  • To cancel, log in to your account at license.nregabot.com, go to "My Account", and click "Disable Auto-Renewal".
  • Cancellation stops future billing. Your license will remain active until the end of the current paid period.
  • We do not provide refunds for partial months or unused time if you cancel early.

3. Technical Issues & Exceptions

In the rare event of a technical error (e.g., you were charged but did not receive a key, or a double charge occurred), please contact us immediately.

We will investigate the issue and, if the error is on our end or with our payment processor, we will process a refund or extend your validity accordingly.

4. Contact Us

If you have any issues with payments or cancellations, please contact us at: nregabot@gmail.com
